Research projects

  • 2016-2020 PIA "65 Millions d'observateurs" project: A MNHN national project dedicated to create "tools" to facilitate and extend participation to citizen science projects related to Biodiversity study
  • 2015-2020 CPER CeSGO project: A INRIA national project with the Brittany region to build the first French e-Science centre in Rennes Bioinformatics Network for NGS Cancer Diagnosis
  • 2012-2015 e-Biogenouest project: A first e-science approach dedicated to Life sciences labs from western France. Financial supports by Brittany and Pays de la Loire Regions through the Biogenouest core facilities network
  • Participation to several projects :
    • Since 2013 French Institute of Bioinformatics PIA national project, corresponding to the French Elixir node
    • 2014-2016 UEB C@mpus innovative project dedicated to Virtual Research Environment concept transposition on several domains as mechanical or electronical research
    • since 2013 National INCa project dedicated to the creation of a French Bioinformatics Network for NGS Cancer Diagnosis
